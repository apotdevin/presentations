Exchanges (Kraken, Coinbase, ...)

Exchanges (Kraken, Coinbase, ...)

  • Ease of use
  • Trade Fiat with BTC
  • Regulated
  • Security is in their hands
  • They hold the key. If they dissappear/go bankrupt/get hacked/don't like you, you might lose your Bitcoin

Mobile/Desktop Apps

Mobile/Desktop Apps

  • Ease of use
  • Security is in your hands
  • Hold your own keys
  • Constantly connected to the internet (Hot Wallet)

Hardware

Hardware

  • Not that easy to use
  • Security is in your hands
  • Hold your own keys
  • Not connected to the internet (Cold Wallet)

Physical

Physical

  • Not really usable
  • Security is in your hands
  • Very good to store backups of your keys

My favorites

  • Exchange: Kraken
  • Mobile App: Samourai (Android), Bluewallet (iOS)
  • Desktop App: Exodus, Electrum and Specter (Needs more setup)
  • Hardware: Coldcard, Cobo Vault
  • Physical: Metal Seed Backup https://estudiobitcoin.com/fabrica-tu-billetera-fria-con-arandelas/

To take into account

  • Try to always have control over your keys. Not your keys, not your coins.
  • Don't store a lot of money in hot wallets
  • The most secure option is a hardware wallet with a physical backup
  • The #1 reason for loss of funds is human error
  • ALWAYS HAVE BACKUPS OF YOUR KEYS/PINS/PASSWORDS
